# Wine Styles
## Still table wines
- red or white
	- dry (0-6g/L residual sugar)
	- off-dry (9-24g/L RS)
## Specialty Wines
- sparkling wines
	- wine with carbon dioxide bubbles
		- secondary fermentation
		- added
		- classified by method and sweetness level
- sweet still wines
	- dessert-style wines
	- classified by grape treatment or processing

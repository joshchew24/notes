# Smell - Olfactory system
- aromas or flavors
	- volatile, soluble in mucus
	- bind to olfactory receptors in olfactory bulb
	- cause signal transmission to brain
- two routes to olfactory bulb
	- orthonasally (aroma)
	- retronasally (flavour)

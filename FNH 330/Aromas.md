# Aromas
- not added to wine
- varietal aroma comes from grape
- non-varietal aroma comes from fermentation process
- comes from
	- grape skins
		- pulp, to a lesser extent
	- fermentation
	- processing
		- oak aging
		- malolactic fermentation
		- etc.
- depend on
	- grape variety
	- ripeness of grape
	- fermentation style and temperature
